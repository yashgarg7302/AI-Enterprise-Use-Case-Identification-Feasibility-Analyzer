# AI Enterprise Use-Case Identification & Feasibility Analyzer

A consulting-oriented AI decision-support system that identifies high-impact enterprise AI use cases and evaluates their feasibility based on business constraints.

This project simulates the early-stage AI consulting discovery phase by combining structured analytical scoring with domain-specific knowledge to recommend practical AI transformation initiatives.

---

## 🚀 Project Overview

Organizations often struggle to determine:

- Which AI initiatives align with business goals?
- Are those initiatives realistically feasible?
- What is the expected ROI?
- What risks are involved?
- What implementation roadmap should be followed?

This system addresses those challenges by:

- Mapping business pain points to AI use cases
- Calculating feasibility using structured scoring logic
- Estimating expected business impact
- Performing AI maturity assessment
- Generating consulting-style executive summaries
- Providing structured risk evaluation

### 2️⃣ Structured Feasibility Scoring Model

Feasibility Score is calculated using weighted evaluation:

Feasibility =  
(0.4 × Data Readiness) +  
(0.3 × Budget Readiness) +  
(0.3 × Timeline Suitability)

Each use case is further adjusted using complexity weights to simulate real-world consulting constraints.

---

### 3️⃣ Business Impact (ROI) Estimation

Based on objective selection:
- Cost Reduction → 10–20% operational savings
- Revenue Growth → 8–15% revenue uplift
- Customer Experience → 15–25% satisfaction improvement
- ESG Strategy → Sustainability positioning enhancement

---

### 4️⃣ AI Maturity Assessment

Based on aggregated feasibility score.

### 6️⃣ Executive-Level Summary Generation
